Character Concepts

Developed a cohesive cast of characters through thumb-nailing, lineup, turn around and concept art for a storyboard. Clip Studio Paint was used for all digital art for this project. Research for the project included finding reference material for wings, digitigrade legs, antlers, and claws.

Character Turn Around

An issue that came up when starting to develop the turn around was that the line up sketch of the winged character's clothing did not make sense when expanded into three dimentions. The solution became re-designing the aspects that were not working, and to add or enhance what was already there.
